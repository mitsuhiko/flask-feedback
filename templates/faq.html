Frequently Asked Questions

What's the purpose of this website?

The guys from Mozilla did an experiment during the deployment of the Firefox 4 betas where they added a button into the toolbar with a happy and a sad face. Upon clicking that button the user was taken to a website that asked him in less than 140 characters about their experience with Firefox.

While we cannot integrate that into Flask in the same way because we do not have any kind of user interface that could accomodate such a button, we however encourage users to send us little messages about their experience with Flask.

Why is this not on the main Flask website?

For the very simple reason that this is an experient on its own and might be shut down without further notice. We haven't done anything like that before and are not yet sure about the consequences.

Will you censor here?

Unless hateful comments or spam end up on this website we will not remove any of them. The raw data is available for everyone who is interested.

Why does this website require JavaScript?

For the very simple reason that we currently use JavaScript as a simple method of preventing spam. At least that is the motivation, if the efforts pay off we can't say yet.

Is the source of this website available?

Indeed it is. You can download it from github:
mitsuhiko/flask-feedback
